::Risk For Other Directed Violence::

::Definition::
 Susceptible to behaviors in which an individual demonstrates that he or she can be physically, emotionally, and/or sexually harmful to others.

::Note:: 
NANDA has separated the diagnosis of Violence into its two elements: “other-directed” and “self-directed.” However, the interventions in general address both situations and have been left in one block following the definitions and supporting data of those two diagnoses.
